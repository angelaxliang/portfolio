Art has been an important part of my life since young. I have practiced various traditional mediums – including oil painting, sketching, and Chinese painting – and eventually turned to digital means. My experience in art helps inform my design practices, including color theory, composition, and conveying a narrative.

Here are my original characters Lilith and Eve, inspired by the mythology of the Garden of Eden. Lilith, the serpent, is an embodiment of temptation for Eve, whose inner desires and longings are awaken.
